# Gate 1 System
## QA Team Training Manual
### Version 1.0

---

## 1. Purpose

This manual is written ONLY for QA Team members.

Your role is quality correction, not investigation or surveillance.

---

## 2. Your Role as QA

You are responsible for:
- Reviewing reported issues only
- Confirming whether issues are valid
- Helping camera/interview teams fix problems
- Providing resolution notes

You do NOT:
- Browse clean videos
- Search by names or personal details
- Edit or rename media files
- Access non-issue content

---

## 3. QA Review Workflow

### Standard Process

1. **Open** QA dashboard
2. **Review** reported issue clips
3. **Assess** the issue validity
4. **Prepare** offline QA package if needed
5. **Move** to field if required
6. **Play** issue clip as evidence
7. **Confirm** fix with team
8. **Add** resolution notes
9. **Mark** as resolved

---

## 4. Issue-Only Access

### What You CAN See
- Videos flagged with issues
- Issue metadata (type, severity, reporter)
- Resolution history

### What You CANNOT See
- Clean footage without issues
- Personal search (name, region, condition)
- Full media library

**This protects privacy and maintains focus.**

---

## 5. Offline QA Review

Use offline mode when:
- Network is unavailable
- Field verification is needed
- Evidence must be shown on-site

### Offline Package Properties
- ✅ Encrypted
- ✅ Watermarked
- ✅ Time-limited (24-48 hours)
- ✅ Auto-deleted after expiry

### How to Use
1. Select issue for offline review
2. Click "Prepare Offline Package"
3. Wait for download
4. Review offline
5. Add notes when back online
6. Package auto-deletes

---

## 6. Issue Types Reference

| Type | Description | Common Cause |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| No Audio | Complete audio missing | Mic off/disconnected |
| Low Audio | Audio barely audible | Mic too far/low gain |
| Blurry | Video out of focus | Camera not focused |
| Shaky | Unstable footage | No tripod/movement |
| Cut Interview | Interview incomplete | Recording stopped early |
| Filename Error | Wrong naming format | Editor mistake |
| Duplicate | Same content twice | Re-copy error |

---

## 7. Resolution Notes

### Good Resolution Notes
- "Audio confirmed present after re-check"
- "File re-copied from SD, audio now working"
- "Duplicate removed, original retained"
- "Editor re-named with correct format"

### Bad Resolution Notes
- "Fixed" (too vague)
- "OK" (no detail)
- "Done" (uninformative)

**Be specific. Notes are part of the audit trail.**

---

## 8. Escalation from QA

Escalate to Admin when:
- Issue cannot be resolved
- Pattern of repeated issues detected
- Technical system failure
- Policy violation suspected

---

## 9. QA Conduct

- Focus on the issue, not the person
- Be factual and objective
- Avoid blame language
- Confirm visually before marking resolved
- Document everything

---

## 10. What You Must NOT Do

- ❌ Browse clean media
- ❌ Search by personal details
- ❌ Download original files
- ❌ Share clips outside system
- ❌ Skip verification
- ❌ Resolve without checking

---
